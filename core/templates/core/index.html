About Us

Creativia is an IT convergence firm. Using technological tools, we work together to design, build, and create the solution to your problem. We specialize in innovative research that involves the building and development of smart energy grids, smart homes, smart factories, and fine tuning additive manufacturing processes. Our team incorporates operations, technology, and marketing skills, with which we ensure to assist clients in handling new projects.
Our Goal

We hope to be able to offer unique solutions to countries all over the globe. At all times, we are committed to exceeding the expectations of our clients by providing solutions that solve problems from the grass root up.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be a leading platform and center for next-generation research and development in the metaverse. We want to be judged as such by our clients, employees, shareholders, and even the communities we work with.

Technical Support

Over the past years of experience with industrial systems, network applications, and AI platforms, we discovered a pattern. There are patterns in every problem sphere and across different industries with similar issues. Luckily, a similar pattern was discovered in the solutions that resolve these problems. These solutions are made up of tiny building blocks such as applications and add-ons that help to realize the desired result. Our consultants' previous experience includes working on software development, virtual reality, augmented reality, 3D-printing, wireless communication, artificial intelligence, networking, etc. With this vast experience, our team is at the beck and call of any problem and are highly trained to handle every project.

Technology Exchange

Creativia holds expertise in conceptualizing, designing, and sourcing AI-based solutions for different industrial processes. At Creativia, we strongly believe that engineers are required to develop a sustainable and industrial society. Thus, we wish to empower the workforce of client companies using a people-centric technology exchange model. Feel free to reach out to us for your needs and we will endeavor to do our best in a cost-effective and timely manner.

Human Resource

Group collaboration is something Creativia excels at, empowered by diverse expertise and shared initiatives. At Creativia, we have a number of industry and academic veterans who have a wide range of experience. We believe in innovation and strive to bring innovation into every project.
