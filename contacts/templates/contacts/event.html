Workshop on Metaverse and Intelligent Transportation

Metaverse refers to a digitally simulated environment that is connected to the physical environment. Africa and indeed Nigeria is amongst the top users of online platforms. Thus, research into the applicability, technologies, and possible challenges of metaverse platform deployment in Africa is apt currently. The rising population in African countries (Nigeria inclusive) demands a planned and tech-based transportation systems to ease the movement of human and resources. The Nigerian government has taken several initiatives in enhancing the transportation sector especially the rail transportation system recently.
